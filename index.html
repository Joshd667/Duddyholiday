Wexford Family Adventure

Your Unforgettable 6-Day Summer Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Beach Bliss

Settle in and hit the sand!

  • 🏠

    Check-In:

    Settle into **Upton Court Hotel** or a cozy seaside cottage.

  • 🥤

    Cool Off:

    Grab smoothies at **Kate’s Coffee Shop & Deli**.

  • 🏖️

    Beach Afternoon:

    Paddle and build sandcastles at **Morriscastle Beach**.

  • 🍽️

    Family Dinner:

    Enjoy outdoor seating at **Lawler’s of Morriscastle**.

Day 2: Wildlife Wonders

An adventure with furry and scaly friends!

  • 👟

    Morning Stroll:

    Take the buggy-friendly **Kilmuckridge-Morriscastle Trail**.

  • 🦙

    Wildlife Park:

    Visit **Secret Valley Wildlife Park** and try pony rides!

  • 🐍

    Reptile Zone:

    Don't miss the park's new and exciting reptile area.

  • 🍦

    Sweet Treat:

    Grab local ice cream in Gorey at **Partridges**.

Day 3: Wells House Adventure

A full day of magic, animals, and history.

  • 🧚

    Fairy Trails:

    Explore the magical woodland trails at **Wells House & Gardens**.

  • 🏹

    Try Archery:

    Test your aim with a fun archery session on site.

  • ☕

    Lunch Stop:

    Refuel with tasty treats at the on-site **Brambles Café**.

  • 🌸

    Optional Detour:

    Visit the serene **Glenavon Japanese Garden**.

Day 4: Castles & Crafts

History, nature, and a splash of creativity.

  • 🏰

    Enniscorthy Castle:

    A quick drive to explore a historic landmark.

  • 🚲

    Cycle the Sandhills:

    Enjoy a ride or walk around the **Tinnaberna Sandhills**.

  • 🎨

    Get Crafty:

    Try pottery painting at **Claymazing** or **Art It** in Gorey.

  • 🍕

    Picnic Dinner:

    Grab takeaway pizza for a relaxing garden picnic.

Day 5: Festivities & Free Play

Enjoy local markets and lazy beach days.

  • 🛍️

    Summer Markets:

    Browse the weekend market stalls in nearby towns.

  • 🪁

    Beach Chill Time:

    Fly a kite or play frisbee at **Ballinoulart Beach**.

  • 🎁

    Pick Up Souvenirs:

    Grab a memento or a new book from a local store.

  • 🖍️

    Kids' Crafts:

    Enjoy drop-in craft sessions at **The Greenhouse Café**.

Day 6: Farewell Fun

One last adventure before heading home.

  • 🎭

    Catch a Show:

    See a family-friendly play at the **Kilmuckridge Drama Festival**.

  • 🍝

    Farewell Brunch:

    Enjoy a final meal at **Da Paolo Italian Restaurant**.

  • 🚶‍♂️

    One Last Stroll:

    Stop by **Wexford town** on the way home for a walk.

  • 🍨

    The Final Ice Cream:

    It's a tradition! One last scoop before the journey ends.
